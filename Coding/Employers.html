Welcome to the Career Portal of Wits University

Here you can place directly and free of charge internships,job ads, traineeships and events which are exclusively visible for students and graduates from Wits University.

  • Register via e-mail and create your recruiter profile.
  • After confirming your password via e-mail, you will have permanent access to your recruiter back-office.
  • Place internships, job offers or traineeships.
  • Prior to publication, Student Career Services will validate all content. This may take max. 3 working days to process.
  • Login anytime to manage, adjust, duplicate or resubmit your offers.
  • Stay up to date by gaining insight into the views per offer.
  • Job ads will be be automatically archieved 2 months after publication when choosing 'as soon as possible' or 1 month after the start date of the offer.

Choose the application procedure when uploading your offer.

  1. Per e-mail:
    Receive a notification via e-mail for every applicant and manage the applicants and resumes via your back-office.
  2. Via external website:
    Redirect students to your company website via Career Portal to start the application process.

More visibility? Would you like to inspire students and graduates with more information and enlarge your company's visibility?

Are there company events you want to promote or would you like to use registration tools for visitors?

Interested to get in touch with study- and student associations?

Other questions? Please contact Student Career Services via studentscareerservices@wits.ac.za


Be aware: this platform is only available to students and recent graduates of Wits University. Job offers requiring >5 years of work experience will not be posted.
